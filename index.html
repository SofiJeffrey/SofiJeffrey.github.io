Discovering New Math

Mathematics is an art worth studying for the joy and beauty of knowledge. In the Mathematical humanities, I analyze art through the lens of math, discovering nontrivial patterns in geometry. Fusing Linear Algebra and Complex Analysis, I study current and pressing open questions on Matrix functions.

Art Through a Mathematical Lens

Using the method of mathematical envelopes, we defined and related the parabolic curves which arc across the geometric drawings of Emma Kunz

Report on Separation Processes

Equilibrium chemistry can be leveraged to separate multi-phased systems. I have compiled a comprehensive manual on processes exploiting this tequnique and how to analyze them

Modeling Sour Beer Fermentation

Certain species of 'sour' yeast produce lactic acid in addition to ethanol during the fermentation process. I work to extend existing kinetic models of beer fermentation to capture this acidic behavior
